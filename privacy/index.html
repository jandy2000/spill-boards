Privacy Policy & Support

Privacy

Taro Pop collects no personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

Your progress — cards, coins, and merch — is stored on your device and, if you use iCloud, backed up to your personal iCloud account, which only you can access.

Leaderboards are provided by Apple Game Center under Apple's privacy policy; playing without Game Center works fully.

The daily board is fetched anonymously from a static file — the request contains no identifiers.
